Project 4 (Optional): Crochet and Crochet Survey

This is a project I have been working on in my own time. I began to crochet again after a decade of not doing so. However, before I only knew one or two crochet skills and never had done a plushie. Now, I have done 5 turtle plushies in two sizes, 1 cow plushie, and 1 penguin plushie.

Because I post my progress on social media, people have been constantly reaching out to me to sell my crochet plushies. As a result, I decided to do something about it and began working on a survey. The survey lists the plushies I have in stock and images of them, and customers simply have to select which one they want to reserve and buy it from me - similar to a website.

The most effective part of this project so far is all of the new skills I have been learning and improving on, and falling in love with an old hobby of mine all over again.
